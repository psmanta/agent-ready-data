# AI Ready Data Experiments

A systematic exploration of the six pillars of AI-ready data through controlled experiments with agentic AI systems.

## The Six Pillars

1. **Authoritative**: Agents act on truth, not noise
2. **Comprehensive**: Agents see the big picture, not fragments
3. **Contextual**: Agents understand your business meaning, not generic interpretation
4. **Timely**: Agents see the world as it is, not as it was
5. **Secure**: Agents stay in-bounds and keep your data there
6. **Responsible**: Agents act in ways that are fair, governed, and defensible
